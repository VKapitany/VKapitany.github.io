Running Fury

controls:
  • ASD / arrow keys - movement
  • Space - jump
interactions:
  • move forward > score up
  • move red ball off map > score up
  • get pill with yellow sparks > score up
  • get pill with blue sparks > time slows
  • hit duck > score down
  • hit watermelon > die
  • fall off map > die
